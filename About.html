Firm History


SAA Architects Engineers first began its operation in 1957. Upon graduating from the University of California-Berkeley, Jim Tomblinson, returned to Flint, Michigan to begin a practice with fellow architect Cliff Gibbs. In just four years the firm produced four award winning projects thus helping to recognize the firm as a leader in the profession.

Attracted by the innovative quality of work emanating from what would become known as SAA, Jerry Harburn joined the firm in 1961. Harburn, having already distinguished himself at the University of Michigan by achieving the highest academic average among architectural graduates, had just completed his architectural internship with renowned architect, Eero Saarinen.

Through commitment to providing quality service and design excellence, Tomblinson Harburn Associates received more design awards and developed a solid, loyal client base to which SAA continues to serve with excellence.
